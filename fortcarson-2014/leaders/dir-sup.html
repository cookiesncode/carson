Mr. Wally Wininger

Director

United States Army Forces Command

Mission Support Element


Biography

Mr. Wally Wininger entered the United States Military Academy (USMA) in July 1975.

After graduation, he served the Army faithfully wherever called over the course of a 30 year active duty military career. As an infantry officer, he had the privilege of leading Soldiers at all levels from platoon through brigade. He has served in various capacities on company, battalion, brigade, division, Department of the Army, and combatant command assignments.

He holds a Bachelor of Science Degree from the USMA as well as Master Degrees from Central Michigan University & the Army War College. In June of 2009 he was appointed to his current duties as Director, Mission Support Element, Fort Carson, CO.
